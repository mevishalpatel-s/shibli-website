About Our Institution

Founded in 1883, Shibli National College stands as one of the oldest and most respected educational institutions in Azamgarh. Named after the renowned Islamic scholar Shibli Nomani, the college carries forward his legacy of promoting Islamic education and modern learning.

With over a century of academic excellence, we have nurtured thousands of students who have gone on to become leaders, professionals, and pillars of society. Our institution blends traditional Islamic values with contemporary educational practices.

We are committed to providing holistic education that develops intellectual, moral, and character development of our students. Our world-class infrastructure, experienced faculty, and inclusive atmosphere make us a premier choice for higher education.
